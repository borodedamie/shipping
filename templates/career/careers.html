Shipping Clerk:

The Shipping Clerk/Driver is responsible for Packaging and delivering daily shipments and orders on-time. In addition, the individual will pick-up/deliver materials, supplies, etc. as needed.

Job Functions:

Coordinate daily with Quality, Accounting, Shipping, and/or Production to priortize delivery of orders based on the daily shipment list.
Prepare all completed PCB orders for shipment and create packing slips.
Create labels for shipments using FedEx or USP shipping software.
Promptly deliver orders and pick-up outside services i.e materials, supplies, etc.
Handle all boards properly with caution to avoid damages.
Read travelers and understand outside service requirements.
Respond to internal/external customers in a professional and timely manner.
Safely operate company vehicle while following local laws and regulation

Qualifications:

To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skills, and/or abilities required.

  • High School Diploma or GED.
  • Two (2) years experience in shipping/driving preferred.
  • Good sense of directions and abiliy to read and understand maps.
  • Ability to read/understand travelers and outside service requirements is a plus.
  • knowledgeable in Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook.
  • Ability to lift up to 25 pounds maximum and stand/walk throughout the day.
  • Valid driver's license is required.
